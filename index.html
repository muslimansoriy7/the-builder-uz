BUILD

Mahsulot qurish

B1 Yadro: 7 ko'nikma
PRD, CustDev, Prioritization, UI/UX sense, Stakeholder, Metrics, System Thinking — AI qila olmaydigan asl qiymat.
B2 Vibe Coding
Cursor, Lovable, Bolt — 80% AI tezligi, 20% sizning nazoratingiz. Qayerda to'xtaydi va nega.
B3 Mahsulotni loyihalash
MVP, funksiyalarni saralash, wireframe, user flow — kod yozishdan oldin.
B4 Backend va API
REST, JSON, SQL, RLS, Webhook — ma'lumot qayerda va qanday saqlanishi.
MARKET

Marketing va sotish

M1 Marketing tadqiqotlari
Bozor, mijoz (CustDev), raqobat (SWOT), mahsulot tahlili — strategiya uchun poydevor.
M2 Strategiya va Brand
Pozitsiyalanish, Marketing Mix, JTBD, CJM, UTP, SOSTAC modeli.
M3 Performance Marketing
Sales Funnel, Meta/Google Ads, sotuvchi kopirayting, ROAS/ROMI, media plan.
M4 Distribution va B2B
Personal Brand, Cold DM + Case Study, Demo-First, retainer modeli, monetizatsiya.
SCALE

Tizim va o'sish (TPM darajasi)

S1 System Design
Caching, Index, Replication, Load Balancer, Microservices, CAP teoremasi — chuqur texnik.
S2 God Mode Toolkit
Gemini, Claude Code, Cursor, Supabase, Vercel, n8n — bir kishi = butun jamoa.
S3 SDLC va xavfsizlik
Discovery→Deploy haftalarda. Security by Default, Documentation is Oxygen.
S4 Daromad va agentlik
Loyiha, retainer, konsalting, white-label, infoprodukt, SaaS — pul ishlash modellari.
